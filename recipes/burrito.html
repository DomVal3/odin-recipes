Burrito Recipe

Description

A simple burrito that sone can enjoy in less than 30 minutes at home. It uses completely fresh ingredients and is tasty enough to satisfy one hungry person. You dont need to be a master chef in order to complete this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of Carne Asada
  • 1/8 of White Onion
  • 1/2 an avocado
  • Handful of cilantro
  • 1/2 of a Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Burrito sized Flour tortilla
  • (Optionl) Salsa

Steps

  1. Take your tomato and cut it and half, but make sure to dice it as fine as you want it to be.
  2. Take the handful of cilantro and begin cutting into fine pieces along the cutting board.
  3. Grab an onion and cut a sliver of it about the size of 1/8 of its total size (If you like onion you can add more)
  4. Pull apart as much lettuce as you like to be used in your burrito
  5. Set aside all ingredient while you cook your meat and tortilla
  6. Either Grill your 1/2 lb of carne asada or fry it for about 2 minutes on each side on high temp
  7. While the meat is cooking, warm up the tortilla on a non-stick pan on medium high heat
  8. Chop the meat up after cooking it into tiny pieces and place it into the tortilla
  9. Cut avocado in half and scoop the insides with a spoon and place them in the burrito
  10. Add the rest of the ingredients you cut up earlier into the burrito and then fold it up (add salsa if you have any)

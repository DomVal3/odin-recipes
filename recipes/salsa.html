Red Tomato Salsa

Description

This tomato based salse is very simply but bring a lot of flavor and a spicy kick to whatever you are eating it with. It is also good enough to be finished in one sitting with just a bag of tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 5 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/3 of an Onion
  • 2 Jalepenos
  • 3 Serranos
  • Cilantro
  • 1 clove of Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes in half and place them on grill with the Jalepenos and Serranos
  2. Cook the tomatoes for about 5 mins of each sice on medium high heat and the peppers for only 2-3 mins on each side
  3. Once toasted place 3 tomatoes, 1 Jalepeno, 3 Serranos, 1 clove of Garlic, and half a tablespoon of salt into a blender
  4. Blend it til it liquifies nicely on high
  5. Place the remaining tomatoes, Jalepenos. and handful of cilantro into the blender
  6. Control the blending with pulse and make the salsa as thick as you would like it
  7. Dice up the 1/3 of an Onion and place them into the salsa and let it sit for 1 hour in order to express all the flavors

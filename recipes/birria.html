Beef Birria Tacos

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of Beef short ribs
  • 1lb of Oxtail
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 2 Chiles de Arbol
  • 4 Chiles de Guajille
  • 4 Chiles de Ancho
  • 3 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 1 Tablespoons of Black Pepper
  • Half a stick of Cinnamon
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cumin
  • 10 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 2 litres of Beef Stock
  • 3 Bay leaves

Instructions

  1. Rehydrate the chiles by boiling them in a pot for 30 minutes.
  2. Dice up the Onion into medium-small pieces and the cloves of garlic into small pieces.
  3. Heat up a pan with the Tomato Sauce and once it starts simmering add the chopped onions and chopped garlic to the pan. Stir for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the ingredients in the saucepan into the blender. Also add the chiles into the blender along with the salt, pepper, cumin, and cinnamon. Blend for 2 minutes on high.
  5. Put the ribs and oxtail into a soup kettle and pour the contents of the blender on top of the meat.
  6. Pour the beef stock in after and then your bay leaves.
  7. Bring it to a boil and then bring it to a light bubbling simmer.
  8. Let it cook for about 3 1/2 hours.
  9. Add to tortillas for tacos, adding garnishes such as onions and cilantro to complete the meal
  10. Have a small bowl to pour the Birria stock into. It adds flavor to the tacos if you dip them into it.
